A little about me...

my background and how I got to where I am now.


I’m the builder of this blog. I’m also a part time student, career changer, data analyst, and web developer. Let me explain.

I’m originally from Germany and grew up in a military family. I’ve lived in Texas, Tennessee, New York, and New Hampshire, but right now I live outside of Boston, MA. I like it here and plan on sticking around for a while.

Originally I went to school for Psychology which led me to a fulfilling career in social work. After spending seven years in the industry I decided to move on to something new. So I went back to school for finance. During that time a friend and I started a business doing freelance web design for local businesses. We had a blast together, writing code and teaching ourselves new development frameworks. Not to mention our work paid for a chunk our respective degrees. Shortly after I graduated with an MBA, I landed a job as a financial planner. It was great in that I combined all of those years in social work with the my new financial skills. It was also a grind. Long hours and a lot of the wrong kind of pressure made me reevaluate my career. I am very happy that I did!

Turns out after all of that searching, I figured out the thing I like most was the thing that has been a hobby all along. It was web development! Throughout it all, the one thing that remained constant is how much I read about and dabbled in writing code, learning about data, and building websites. Now I’m taking a step further and going back to school for a computer science degree. I'm finally making a career out of it!
